# Workflow Notes

## Content Creation Workflow
1. Receive product or campaign brief
2. Identify target audience
3. Build 3-5 hook angles
4. Generate caption options
5. Refine tone for B2B or consumer-facing use
6. Prepare final script and supporting text overlays

## Goals
- Speed up content production
- Improve consistency across outputs
- Create reusable prompt systems
- Reduce time spent rewriting drafts

## Current Focus
- Retail-focused content workflows
- Product storytelling systems
- Short-form video scripting
- Campaign planning support
